Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • milk powder
  • ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and ghee to form a soft dough.
  2. Roll the dough into small balls.
  3. Boil sugar, water, and cardamom to make a syrup.
  4. Fry the balls until golden, then soak them in the syrup.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • milk powder
  • chopped nuts
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup condensed milk with 2 cups milk powder in a pan.
  2. Cook on low heat, stirring until it thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  3. Add cardamom powder and pour into a greased tray.
  4. Let it set, then cut into squares to serve.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cashews
  • Ghee
  • Milk Powder
  • Silver leaf or Vark

Recipe

  1. Soak 1 cup cashews, then grind to a fine powder.
  2. Heat 1 cup sugar with 1/2 cup water until it forms a one-string syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder to the syrup and cook until it thickens.
  4. Roll out the mixture, cut into diamonds, and enjoy

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Rabri
  • Sugar
  • Chopped nuts
  • Paneer

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle, then strain.
  2. Knead chhena and form small balls.
  3. Boil sugar and water, add balls, cook until they puff up.
  4. Soak in sweetened milk and chill before serving.

Mysore Pak

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Besan
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Cook 1 cup besan (chickpea flour) in 1 cup ghee until fragrant.
  2. In a separate pan, make a syrup by dissolving 1 cup sugar in 1/2 cup water.
  3. Gradually add the syrup to the besan mixture, stirring continuously.
  4. Cook until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.

Ladoo

Ingredients

  • Besan
  • Sugar
  • Chopped nuts
  • Ghree

Recipe

  1. Roast 1 cup besan in 1/4 cup ghee until golden.
  2. Make a syrup with 1 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water.
  3. Mix roasted besan with the syrup.
  4. Shape into small balls(ladoos)
